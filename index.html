I approach social media with curiosity, creativity, whimsy and experimentation, with the aim of inspiring a sense of community and wonder. I use analytics and audience insights as a guide to continually improve content outcomes, while maintaining integrity.

I’d begin by learning more about your members and associated stakeholders to develop tailored social media content that supports their needs, alongside promoting ESA's events, publications and activities.

I’ve completed workshops on marketing, so I understand how to establish strong branding guidelines and how to implement them across platforms to increase the recognisability of your organisation.

For example, I’ve always really enjoyed seeing people share their ecology fashions at ESA’s annual conferences.
